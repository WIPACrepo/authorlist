Instructions for updating the author list

If you are not a PI:

Ask your PI to make the changes.

If you are a PI:

Log in to the user management portal at user-management.icecube.aq/institutions and select or deselect users for the IceCube and IceCube-Gen2 author lists.
If you have any questions or are having trouble with access, email help@icecube.wisc.edu.
